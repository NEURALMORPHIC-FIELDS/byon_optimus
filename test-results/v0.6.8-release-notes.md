## What landed

- **`DOMAIN_VERIFIED` trust tier** sits between `VERIFIED_PROJECT_FACT` and `USER_PREFERENCE` in the hierarchy. Carries domain knowledge with full provenance metadata.
- **Operator and domain-ingestion channels can create domain facts** via the new memory-service endpoints (`domain_fact_add`, `domain_fact_revoke`, `domain_fact_list`, `domain_fact_review`, `domain_fact_search`) and the new CLI `byon-orchestrator/scripts/byon-domain.mjs`.
- **Chat / WhatsApp / conversation channels CANNOT create `DOMAIN_VERIFIED`.** Server rejects with HTTP 403. Bench item N3 verified the gate live.
- **Domain facts require provenance.** Server-side mandatory schema:
  `domain`, `jurisdiction`, `subject`, `predicate`, `object`, `retrieved_at`, `effective_from`, `review_after`, `citation`, plus at least one of `source_name` / `source_url` / `source_path`.
- **Expired facts are demoted to `DISPUTED_OR_UNSAFE`.** When `review_after` is in the past, `inferTrustFromHit` reclassifies the fact at recall and surfaces it under `[6]` with a stale-knowledge marker, recommending reverification (bench N4 verified).
- **Revoked facts are not cited as authoritative.** `domain_fact_revoke` flips trust to `DISPUTED_OR_UNSAFE`, adds `__revoked__` + `__disputed__` tags, records audit metadata. Default `list-domain` hides them (bench N7 verified).
- **Wrong jurisdiction does not apply.** Bench N5: a German VAT fact is not used to answer a Romania VAT query.
- **SYSTEM_CANONICAL wins over DOMAIN_VERIFIED.** Bench N8: a domain "internal policy" claiming Executor may have network access is refused ‚Äî air-gap canonical wins.
